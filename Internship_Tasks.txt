In this program you will learn key analytics skills such as:

1. Data wrangling
2. Data visualization
3. Programming skills
4. Statistics
5. Critical thinking
6. Commercial thinking


Task 1
Data preparation and customer analytics
Conduct analysis on your client's transaction dataset and identify customer purchasing behaviours to generate insights and provide commercial recommendations.

Julia, the chips category manager wants to understand the customer segments and who she should be targeting within her category.

two datasets
1. a years worth of potato chips transaction
2. customer data for a region(includes life stage and premium customer segments)

some metrics
1. are customers spending more per chip packet over time?
2. are there more customers than chip purchases?
3. are customers multi-pack buyers?


Steps 
1. data preparation and cleaning
2. deriving extra features like size of packages and brand names


Task2
Experimentation and uplift testing
Extend your analysis from Task 1 to help you identify benchmark stores that allow you to test the impact of the trial store layouts on customer sales.

Task 3
Analytics and commercial application
Use your analytics and insights from Task 1 and 2 to prepare a report for your client, the Category Manager.
